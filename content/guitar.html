Guitar Practice

As much as I enjoy playing guitar, I really don't have the time these days to maintain my technique as much as I would like. Thus I am collecting together a series of short excercises that can be performed in very short periods of time to help maintain and improve fundemental guitar technique. Once you get them down, these excercises can be done passively, while watching TV for instance. All music on this page is rendered using the jTab library. Unless I came up with them myself, the origin of any given excercise can be navigated to by clicking on the excercise name.

Linear Warmup

Alternate pick, increasing tempo as comfortable.

$E 1 2 3 4 $A 1 2 3 4 | $D 1 2 3 4 $G 1 2 3 4 | $B 1 2 3 4 $e 1 2 3 4 ||
$e 5 4 3 2 $B 5 4 3 2 | $G 5 4 3 2 $D 5 4 3 2 | $A 5 4 3 2 $E 5 4 3 2 ||

Continue this pattern until around fret 12 and come back down.

Linear Confusion Warmup

Modification of above. Alternate pick, increasing tempo as comfortable.

$E 1 2 3 4 $A 2 3 4 1 | $D 3 4 1 2 $G 4 1 2 3 | $B 1 2 3 4 $e 2 3 4 1 ||
$e 4 5 2 3 $B 5 2 3 4 | $G 2 3 4 5 $D 3 4 5 2 | $A 4 5 2 4 $E 5 2 3 4 ||

Continue this pattern until around fret 12 and come back down.
