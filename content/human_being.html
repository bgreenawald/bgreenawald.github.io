Me

This page is dedicated to all things me.

Musician

Music, both writing and playing, has been a cathartic hobby of mine for almost a decade now. I started playing guitar in middle school, which remains my primary instrument. I casually picked up piano a few years later and although I am competent, my musical theory and music reading abilities need some work. I also have or have ready access to a mandolin, a bass guitar, a ukelele, a synthesizer, and a drum set, all of which I mostly noodle around on. Even though I have written music for many years, I have only recently gotten more serious about composing and recording the things that I have written. As of now, music continues to be something I do for myself but may share some of it on this site in the future!

Modder

I am a big fan video games. I'm a big fan of tweaking things to my liking. So it's no surprise that I am also a big fan of video game modding. At some point or another, I have had a custom modded version of every Super Smash Bros. game (except SSBM 64), with the biggest project being a Smash 4 mod, titled Super Smash Bros. SE.. We have released the Beta but the project is currently on hiatus because my co-modder moved to Seattle and school for me has taken priority. But as the modding community discovers more and more about the game, I hope to update the mod accordingly.

Gamer

After reading the above, it should come as no surprise that I also consider myself a gamer, with the Smash Bros series and its derivatives being my all time favorites. I don't play them all that much as I do consider them a low priority in my life, but getting together with a group of friends to just hang out and play for awhile remains one of my favorite activities. You can find me on Steam, username S0MA (10 points to anyone who gets the reference). The only game I am currently active on is Rivals of Aether where I main Zetterburn and Etalus.
