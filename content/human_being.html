Me

This page is dedicated to all things me! It including glimpses into how I spend my free time and some of my favorite things.

Musician

Music, both writing and playing, has been a cathartic hobby of mine for almost a decade now. I started playing guitar in middle school, which remains my primary instrument. I casually picked up piano a few years later and although I am competent, my musical theory and music reading abilities need some work. I also have or have ready access to a mandolin, a bass guitar, a ukelele, a synthesizer, and a drum set, all of which I mostly noodle around on. Even though I have written music for many years, I have only recently gotten more serious about composing and recording the things that I have written. As of now, music continues to be something I do for myself but may share some of it on this site in the future! Until then, my lyrics can be found here.

Modder

I am a big fan video games. I'm a big fan of tweaking things to my liking. So it's no surprise that I am also a big fan of video game modding. At some point or another, I have had a custom modded version of every Super Smash Bros. game (except SSBM 64), with the biggest project being a Smash 4 mod, titled Super Smash Bros. SE. We have released the Beta but the project is currently on hiatus because my co-modder moved to Seattle and work for me has taken priority. But as the modding community discovers more and more about the game, I hope to update the mod accordingly.

Gamer

After reading the above, it should come as no surprise that I also consider myself a gamer, with the Smash Bros series and its derivatives being my all time favorites. I don't play them all that much as I do consider them a low priority in my life, but getting together with a group of friends to just hang out and play for awhile remains one of my favorite activities. You can find me on Steam, username S0MA (10 points to anyone who gets the reference). The only game I am currently active on is Rivals of Aether where I main Zetterburn and Etalus.

Like everyone and their mother, I play a fair bit of Fortnite (tag 'Sοmα').

Of course, video games are not the only kinds of games out there and I enjoy all kinds! Board games, card games, miscellaneous games, and yes, the occasional drinking game. For board games my favorites are Catan (though that game really stretches my attention span), and Dixit. For card games, I like Capitalism, Bohnanza, Codenames, Oh Hell, Polish Poker (not actually sure if that's what it's called), and actual poker (most variants). For miscellaneous games, my sisters and I are voracious Bananagrams players. As a winter break project, we (well, mostly my sisters, but I helped) created custom tiles for Banagrams that stick together using command strips to make board rearrangement easier.

Writer

Writing, outside of songwriting, is one of my biggest love-hate relationships in this world. When I was younger, my English teachers always told me I was a talented wordsmith (now whether this was actually the truth, or just validation given to the well-behaved child remains to be seen), but I honestly never really enjoyed traditional writing styles. Anytime I needed to express myself, it was always more naturally done through songwriting. But sometimes I do have an idea better expressed in either a short story or poem. Occasionally, I will challenge myself to write something every day just to spark creativity and, occasionally, I will stumble across something written during these times and actually like it. And even though most of the times I feel expressed after only a paragraph short story or succinct poem, it is a minor bucket list item of mine to write something more substantial. For now, I have a page of some of my writing here.
