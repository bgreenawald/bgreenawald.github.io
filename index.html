What this site is

I want to begin by giving you an idea of why I am making this site. The ultimate goal of this site is two fold:
  1. Create a central hub for me. As I grow older and my digital presence increases, it is increasingly difficult to keep a consistent and up to date picture of myself across various platforms. This site is meant to serve as the most up to date and thorough resource for the various goings on in my life.
  2. Open sourcing myself. I create a fair amount of media in various forms, most of which I keep to myself. But in an effort to both share more and create more, I want to put my content out there. It doesn't really matter if it's any good or if anyone ever sees it (and part of me hopes nobody does), but just sharing it feels important.
With that in mind, I now encourage you to explore this site and get to know a little more about me. For a good place to start, read my first blog post, which goes into a little more detail of who I am and what this site is all about.
