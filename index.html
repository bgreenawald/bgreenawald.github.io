What this site is

I want to begin by giving you an idea of why I am making this site. The ultimate goal of this site is three fold:
  1. Create a central hub for me. As I grow older and my digital presence increases, it is increasingly difficult to keep a consistent and up to date picture of myself across various platforms. This site is meant to serve as the most up to date and thorough resource for the various goings on in my life.
  2. Open sourcing myself. I create a considerable amount of media in various forms, most of which I keep to myself. But in an effort to both share more and create more, I want to put my content out there. It doesn't really matter if it's any good or if anyone ever sees it (and part of me hopes nobody does), but just sharing it feels like an important step in my development as a person.
  3. Sharing my thoughts. I, like many people, think often. Because I am the only one that has lived my life, I am the only one that has the ability to share my thoughts. It is a growing belief of mine that because of the ease at which a person is able to generate and host content (thanks, Github), it is the responsibility of the individual to share the thoughts and experiences that only they have. I have decided to take a swing at fulfilling that responsibility here.
  4. Keep my web development skills sharp(ish). I have been studying computer science and developing software for over 4 years, and the prospect of front-end software development still makes me cringe. If you've ever wondered what my Achilles heel is, you're looking at it. Me from 5 years ago would never have imagined that my Kryptonite would be Internet markup language, but here was are. That being said, I acknowledge that the skill is valuable, so I am using this site as a means of facing my fear of the front end, in a nice, static setting.
With that in mind, I now encourage you to explore this site and get to know a little more about me. For a good place to start, read my first blog post, which goes into a little more detail of who I am and what this site is all about.
