A summary

At the risk of plagarising my own LinkedIn summary, let me try and give a brief summary of what I'm all about. But first, let me give you an idea of why I am making this site. The ultimate goal behind this site is three fold:

  1. Create a central hub for me. As I grow older and my digital presence increases, it is increasingly difficult to keep a consistent and up to date picture of myself across various platforms. This site is meant to serve as the most up to date and thorough resource for the various goings on in my life.
  2. Sharing my thoughts. I, like many people, think often. Because I am the only one that has lived my life, I am the only one that has the ability to share my thoughts. It is a growing belief of mine that because of the ease at which a person is able to generate and host content (thanks Github), it is the responsiblity of the individual to share the thoughts and experiences that only they have. I have decided to take a swing at fulfilling that responsiblity here.
  3. Keep my web development skills sharp(ish). I have been studying computer science and developing software for over 4 years, and the prospect of front end software development still makes me cringe. If you've ever wondered what my Achilles heal is, you're looking at it. Me from 5 years ago would never have imagined that my Kryptonite would be Internet markup language, but here was are. That being said, I acknowledge that the skill is valuable, so I am using this site as a means to facing my fear of the front end, in a nice, static setting.
